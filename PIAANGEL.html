Welcome to CliniCITE

CliniCITE is a web-based system designed to manage student health records efficiently at your school clinic.

Features:

  • Register students with course information
  • Record clinic visits with symptoms and treatment
  • View and search clinic records in real-time
  • Clean, intuitive interface optimized for desktop and mobile
